Research Directions

AI4Rad Lab builds on four technical pillars to address clinical problems across seven medical domains — from pan-vascular imaging and musculoskeletal quantification to oncology, chronic disease cohorts, emergency imaging, and emerging imaging technologies.

👁️
Vision Foundation Models
Medical visual representation and structured understanding for diagnosis, segmentation, detection, localization, and quantitative parameter extraction across imaging modalities.
🧠
Multimodal Large Language Models
Integrating medical images, radiology reports, clinical history, and knowledge bases for report generation, clinical reasoning, evaluation frameworks, and agentic AI.
🎨
Medical Image Generation
Cross-modal synthesis, low-dose denoising, image enhancement, fast imaging, missing-sequence completion, and downstream diagnostic utility assessment.
🌐
Spatial Intelligence & Physics Simulation
3D spatial modeling, registration, hemodynamic simulation, musculoskeletal mechanics, vascular topology, and physics-consistent quantitative analysis.
